Sign-up!
================================

To sign up to the AWAKEN wind farm wake benchmark, please send an email to nicola.bodini@nrel.gov. 

Participations is limited to people whose institutions are based in countries who are part of the IEA Wind Task 57 'JAM'. Check the `JAM website <https://iea-wind.org/task57/>`_ for more information.
